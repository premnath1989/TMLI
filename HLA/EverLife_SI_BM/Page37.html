The critical illness provision does not cover the following occurrences:
i.     any episode of coronary artery or ischemic heart disease which occurs before the Issue Date or any reinstatement date of the Policy;
ii.Diagnosis of the dread disease other than those specified under item (iii) below within thirty (30) days from the Issue Date or any reinstatement
date, whichever is later;
iii.    Diagnosis of the critical illness specified below within 60 days from the Issue Date or any reinstatement date, whichever is later:

iv.    Other than the first incidence of the critical illnesses.
