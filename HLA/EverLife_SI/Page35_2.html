5. Medical Procedure Coverage
Reimbursement up to 100% of the Rider Sum Assured is payable in the event that Life Assured suffers for the following conditions:
(i) Breast Reconstructive surgery following a mastectomy;
(ii) Facial Re-constructive surgery for face and neck disfigurement due to accident;
(iii) Skin grafting due to accidental burns.

The following situations, conditions or illnesses are excluded:
(a) Breast Reconstructive Surgery following a mastectomy within sixty days (60) days of the Issue Date, Alteration Effective Date or any reinstatement date of the Policy whichever is the later;
(b) Facial Re-constructive surgery for face and neck disfigurement due to accident and Skin grafting due to accidental burns occurring before the Issue Date, Alteration Effective Date or any reinstatement date of this Rider whichever is the later;
(c) any incidence of any of the above stated conditions other than the first incidence;
(d) Death of the Life Assured within thirty (30) days following the date of Breast Reconstructive Surgery following a mastectomy.

6. Hospitalisation due to Robbery
In the event that Life Assured is hospitalised due to robbery, 0.1% of the Rider Sum Assured per day shall be payable for each day of hospitalisation. This benefit is capped at 3 days per event.

7. Accidental Death due to Robbery
In the event of death of Life Assured due to robbery, 100% of the Rider Sum Assured is payable.
