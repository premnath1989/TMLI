Description of Basic Plan Benefits
This is a participating endowment plan with a policy term of years. The premium payment term for this plan is limited to years only.
Death/ Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) Benefit
(a)In the event of death/TPD (prior to attaining age 65) of the Life Assured, the death/TPD benefit equivalent to the sum of the following shall be payable:
(i) of premium paid for Basic Policy (exclude any loading premium) calculated based on an annual mode of payment,
(ii)Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any), and
(iii)Special Terminal Dividend (if any).

(b)For basic policy issued on the life of an unborn child,
(i)In the event of Death of the Life Assured from the commencement date up to thirty (30) days after birth, the amount payable shall be 100% of premium paid for this Basic Policy (exclude any loading premium) calculated based on an annual mode of payment.
(ii)In the event of TPD of the Life Assured from birth until thirty (30) days after birth, the amount payable shall be 100% of premium paid for this Basic Policy (exclude any loading premium) calculated based on an annual mode of payment.
(iii)The coverage of the Life Assured will follow the Death/ TPD benefits as shown in part (a) above from thirty one (31) days after birth onwards.

TPD Benefit will be paid in accordance to the provision as stated in the policy:

Provision for TPD Benefit Limit per Life

Age at TPD (last birthday) TPD Benefit Limit Per Life
Less than 7 RM 100,000
7 to less than 15 RM 500,000
15 to less than 65 RM 3,500,000
Total TPD Benefits per Life payable under all policies insuring the Life Assured shall not exceed the TPD Benefit Limit per Life stated above.
The Total TPD Benefit per Life refers to TPD coverage of all in force policies for each Life Assured at the point of claim event.

Maturity Benefit
The Basic Policy will mature upon survival of the Life Assured at the end of the policy term. A Guaranteed Maturity Benefit equivalent to of premium paid for Basic Policy (exclude any loading premium) calculated based on an annual mode of payment shall be payable in addition to the following:
(i)Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any), and
(ii)Terminal Dividend (if any).
