This rider does not cover the following occurrence:
i.     Pre-Existing Condition.
ii.     All Cancers presenting or diagnosed within the Waiting Period of 90 days.
iii.    All Cancers which are histologically classified as being:
       (a)     Premalignant (for example cervical intraepithelial / CIN I, II, or III, essential thrombocythemia, polycythemia rubra vera) or having either
                 borderline malignancy / low malignant potential and/or;
       (b)     Carcinoma in situ (except Ductal carcinoma in situ breast, Carcinoma in situ cervix and Carcinoma in situ colon).
iv.    All skin Cancers except malignant melanoma with thickness in excess of 1.5 mm thickness or metastatic skin Cancers.
v.     All Cancers associated with HIV infection.
vi.    Unreasonable failure to follow medical treatment.
vii.   All Cancers if Death of the Life Assured occurs within thirty (30) days following the date of diagnosis of Cancer.
