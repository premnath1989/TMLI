Non-Deductible Benefit
The following benefits shall not be subject to deductible:
(a).   Daily Cash amount for hospitalisation due to road accidents on a Malaysian Highway
(b).   Daily Cash allowance for each day confined in a Government Hospital
(c).   Outpatient Cancer Treatment
(d).   Outpatient Kidney Dialysis Treatment
(e).   Emergency Evacuation & Repatriation

Period of Cover and Renewal
This plan is renewable at the option of the Policy Owner until the occurrence of any of the following:
-    non payment of premium or premium not made on time
-    fraud or misrepresentation of material fact during application
-    the Policy is cancelled at the request of the Policy Owner
-    total claims of the Policy have reached the lifetime limit specified (i.e. the Aggregate Term Limit) ,and/or on the death of the Life Assured.
-    the Life Assured ceases to qualify as a dependent based on the definition of the Policy.
-    on the Policy Anniversary Date immediately following the Life Assured attaining the coverage age limit of 100th birthday
-    termination of coverage of this plan for all policies in a certain market and the Company withdraws this plan completely from the market in accordance with the Portfolio Withdrawal Condition

Auto Increase in Annual Limit
The Annual Limit shall increase by 10% of initial Annual Limit every 5 years up to a maximum of 150% of initial Annual Limit, provided no claim is approved within the 5 years period prior to each increase. The Aggregate Term Limit will also increase automatically by the same percentage in tandem with the increase in Annual Limit.

- Annual Limit Waiver
This Benefit provides waiver on the Annual Limit amount. As such, the amount claimable will be subject to the Aggregate Term Limit only. However, any claim amount exceeding the Annual Limit shall subject to a coinsurance amount of 10% on total eligible claims (excluding cost of daily room & board, Daily Cash as a result of hospitalisation due to road accidents on a Malaysian Highway, Daily Cash Allowance at Government Hospital, Home Nursing Care, Emergency Evacuation & Repatriation).
