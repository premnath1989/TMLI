IV) Advancement of Future Guaranteed Yearly Income (GYI)
(i) Policy Owner may advance the next 5 years GYI upon happening of events as listed below; starting from the policy year where the first GYI is payable and provided that the policy has acquired sufficient Net Cash Surrender Value
- Marriage
- Child Birth
- Children's university admission
- Children's graduation/convocation - Children's wedding
- Purchase of new house
- Starting of a business
- Business Disruption
- Policy Owner/ Parents/ Children admitted to hospital
- Parent/children death
- Policy owner diagnosed with critical illness
- Policy owner suffers temporary total disablement due to illness or accident.

The above listed events are not exhaustive and the Company may include more events at its discretion from time to time.

VI) Guaranteed Longevity Cash
Upon the survival of the Life Assured, the Company will pay the Policy Owner a Guaranteed Longevity Cash Benefit which is equivalent to 5 times of the Guaranteed Yearly Income at the end of the Policy Year immediately after the Life Assured attains:
a) the age of seventy (70) years, and
b) the age of eighty (80) years.


VII) Maturity Benefit
The Policy will mature upon survival of the Life Assured at the end of the policy term. A Maturity Benefit equivalent to the sum of the following shall be payable:
(i)    5 times of Guaranteed Yearly Income,
(ii)    Accumulated Guaranteed Yearly Income (if any),
(iii)   Accumulated Guaranteed Longevity Cash (if any),
(iv)  Accumulated Cash Dividend (if any), and
(v)    TerminalDividend(if any)
