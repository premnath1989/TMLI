6. What are the major exclusions under this policy?
Basic Plan
I) Death Benefit
If the Life Assured, whether sane or insane, commits suicide within twelve (12) months from the issue date or the Reinstatement Date, whichever is later, this Policy shall become void and we shall return the premiums and any applicable tax paid without interest after deducting any amount due to us under this Policy.

II) Total & Permanent Disability Benefit
  The policy shall not cover Total & Permanent Disability resulted directly or indirectly;
-     from any self-inflicted bodily injury while sane or insane;
-     from flying in an aircraft (except as an aircrew member of, or as an ordinary fare paying passenger, on a regularly scheduled flight of a commercial airline); or
-     from anything whatsoever while serving as a member of the armed forces, police and paramilitary forces as a result of declared or undeclared war, riots or civil commotion.
